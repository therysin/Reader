 Privacy Policy for FullScreen Reader

Last updated: May 1, 2026

FullScreen Reader is designed as a private fullscreen web reader. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app handles and how it is used.

## Information We Collect

FullScreen Reader does not require you to create an account and does not collect personal information directly from you.

The app may store the following information locally on your device so the reader can function:

- Saved website bookmarks
- Saved page bookmarks
- Recently visited or last opened page
- Reader settings and preferences
- Reader Pro purchase entitlement status
- Optional user scripts or content blocking preferences if you enable those features

## iCloud Sync

If you use Reader Pro and enable iCloud Sync, FullScreen Reader may store your reader data in your personal iCloud account so it can be backed up or restored across your devices.

This may include:

- Bookmarks
- Reader settings
- Saved reader data used for backup and restore

iCloud data is stored through Apple’s iCloud services and is associated with your Apple ID. FullScreen Reader does not operate its own server for iCloud Sync and does not have access to your Apple ID password.

## In-App Purchases

FullScreen Reader offers optional Reader Pro purchases through Apple’s App Store.

Purchases are processed by Apple. FullScreen Reader receives purchase entitlement information from Apple so the app can unlock Reader Pro features. We do not receive or store your payment card details.

## Web Browsing

FullScreen Reader includes a web reader using Apple’s WebKit technology. Websites you visit inside the app may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

FullScreen Reader does not control third-party websites and is not responsible for their content, tracking, cookies, analytics, or privacy practices.

## Data Saver, Userscripts, and Content Blocking

Reader Pro includes optional features such as Data Saver, Userscript support, and Content Blocking.

If enabled, these features may change how websites load or display inside the app. User scripts you add are stored locally on your device, and may also be included in iCloud backup/restore if iCloud Sync is enabled.

You are responsible for any scripts you choose to add.

## Analytics and Tracking

FullScreen Reader does not include third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or cross-app tracking.

The app does not sell your personal information.

## Data Sharing

FullScreen Reader does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

Data may be handled by Apple services when you use App Store purchases, iCloud, TestFlight, crash reporting, or other Apple platform features, subject to Apple’s privacy policies.

## Children’s Privacy

FullScreen Reader is not directed at children under 13. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Your Choices

You can remove locally stored reader data by deleting the app from your device.

You can manage iCloud data through your device’s iCloud settings.

You can manage subscriptions and purchases through your Apple ID settings.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If changes are made, the updated version will be posted with a new “Last updated” date.

## Contact

If you have questions or need support, please open an issue on this repo.
